# Privacy Policy - OkapiFind

**Last Updated: January 17, 2026**

## Introduction

Welcome to OkapiFind. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile and web application.

## Information We Collect

When you use OkapiFind, we may collect the following information:

- **Account Information:** When you sign in with Google or Apple, we collect your email address, name, and profile photo.
- **Location Data:** We collect and store the parking locations you save in the app. This data is stored securely in our database.
- **Device Information:** We collect basic device information such as device type, operating system, and app version for analytics and troubleshooting purposes.
- **Usage Data:** We collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our service, including features used and app performance metrics.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- **Provide Services:** To save and sync your parking locations across devices.
- **Account Management:** To manage your account and provide authentication services.
- **Improve Our Service:** To analyze usage patterns and improve app performance and features.
- **Communication:** To send you important updates about the service (we will never spam you).
- **Security:** To detect and prevent fraudulent activities and ensure platform security.

## Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption:

- All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/SSL.
- Data at rest is stored with encryption.
- We implement strict access controls to protect your information.
- We never sell your personal data to third parties.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

- **Supabase:** For authentication and database services.
- **Google Maps:** For displaying maps and location services.
- **Apple Sign In:** For iOS authentication (iOS only).
- **RevenueCat:** For subscription management.

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

## Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- **Access:** You can access all your saved parking locations within the app.
- **Deletion:** You can delete your saved locations at any time.
- **Account Deletion:** You can request complete account deletion by contacting us.
- **Data Export:** You can request a copy of your data by contacting us.

## Guest Mode

You can use OkapiFind without creating an account in Guest Mode. In Guest Mode:

- Your parking locations are stored only on your device.
- Data will not sync across devices.
- No personal information is collected.
- Local data may be lost if you uninstall the app.

## Children's Privacy

OkapiFind is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
